# Cherenkov Design Firm - Knowledge Base

## Mission
Cherenkov is a multidisciplinary design firm operating at the intersection of architecture, digital noise, and liminal aesthetic. We do not just build; we curate atmospheres. Our goal is to dissolve the boundary between the physical and the digital, creating spaces (both real and virtual) that feel like memories of a future that never happened.

## Core Services
1. **Architectural Design:** Residential and commercial spaces with a focus on brutalist and neo-futurist principles.
2. **Digital Experiential Layers:** Augmented reality interfaces, website atmospheres, and digital installations.
3. **Brand Identity & Myth-Making:** We don't just design logos; we build corporate mythologies and narrative structures.
